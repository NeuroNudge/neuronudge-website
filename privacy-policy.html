Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NeuroNudge collects, uses, and shares information when you use our NeuroNudge mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, we process your transaction through Apple's App Store. We don't collect or store your payment details, which are handled by Apple.
  • User Settings: Your preferences and settings within the app.
  • Game Progress: Your scores, achievements, and statistics to track your progress.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and other technical information.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, which features you use, and how long you use them.
  • Advertising ID: We use your device's advertising identifier to serve you relevant ads through Google Mobile Ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our App.
  • To process your in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • To personalize your experience and track your progress.
  • To serve relevant advertisements.
  • To understand how users interact with our App.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Mobile Ads: Used to display advertisements. Google may collect and process data as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process in-app purchases as described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You can also adjust your device settings to limit ad tracking.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at NeuroNudgeApp@gmail.com, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
