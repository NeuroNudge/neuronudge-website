Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use NeuroNudge.

Last Updated: March 13, 2025

📋
Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NeuroNudge collects, uses, and shares information when you use our NeuroNudge mobile application and website. Please note that while our mobile app collects certain data (as detailed below), our website does not use any non-essential cookies or tracking mechanisms.

📊
Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, we process your transaction through Apple's App Store. We don't collect or store your payment details, which are handled by Apple.
  • User Settings: Your preferences and settings within the app.
  • Game Progress: Your scores, achievements, and statistics to track your progress.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and other technical information.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, which features you use, and how long you use them.
  • Advertising ID: We use your device's advertising identifier to serve you relevant ads through Google Mobile Ads.

🔧
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • To process your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To personalize your experience and track your progress
  • To serve relevant advertisements
  • To understand how users interact with our App

🤝
Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google Mobile Ads: Used to display advertisements. Google may collect and process data as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process in-app purchases as described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

🔒
Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your game progress and personal data are stored securely and are never shared with unauthorized parties.

🌐
Website Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use any non-essential cookies or tracking mechanisms. We only utilize cookies that are strictly necessary for the basic functionality of the site. No personal data or tracking information is collected through these cookies.

⚖️
Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also adjust your device settings to limit ad tracking. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

👶
Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the data.

If you discover that a child under 13 has used our app, please contact us immediately so we can address the situation promptly.

🔄
Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

📧
