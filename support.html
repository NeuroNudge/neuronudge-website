Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How Do I Restore My Purchases?

If you have purchased the Ad-Free Subscription but still see ads, try restoring your purchase:

  1. Open the NeuroNudge App.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases" and wait for confirmation.
  4. If the issue persists, make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
